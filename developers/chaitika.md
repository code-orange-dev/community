## Why This Work Matters

Silent Payments (BIP352) allows Bitcoin users to receive payments at a static, reusable address with no on-chain linkability between payments. It requires no interaction with the sender and creates no on-chain footprint beyond the payment itself. This is among the most significant privacy improvements added to Bitcoin in years.

The infrastructure Chaitika is building — wallet, indexer, and library — is what allows end-users and other developers to actually use Silent Payments. Without this application layer, the protocol exists only as a specification.
