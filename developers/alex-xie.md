## About

Alex Xie is focused on the intersection of Bitcoin and Nostr — the censorship-resistant communication protocol deeply intertwined with Bitcoin's sovereignty stack. Working across key management (local signers, browser-based signers, remote signers), relay infrastructure, NIPs (Nostr Improvement Proposals), and non-social-media Nostr applications, Alex is building toward contributions to Bitcoin's decentralised communication and application layer.

## Focus Areas

- Key management architectures for Bitcoin and Nostr
- Relay infrastructure
- NIPs (Nostr Improvement Proposals) and Kinds
- Nostr VPN concepts
- Nostr Wallet Connect (NWC) — linking Lightning and Nostr

## Why This Matters

Nostr is increasingly the censorship-resistant communication layer for Bitcoin users in authoritarian environments. Key management is one of the most critical and underserved infrastructure problems for Bitcoin self-sovereignty. Nostr Wallet Connect (NWC) is a growing standard linking Nostr and Lightning that needs developer contributions.
