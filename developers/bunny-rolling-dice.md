## About

An electronic engineer by training, Bunny Rolling Dice was involved in one of the earliest and most impactful forms of Bitcoin open-source contribution: translating [BlueWallet](https://github.com/BlueWallet/BlueWallet) into Bahasa Indonesia. BlueWallet is one of Bitcoin's most widely used mobile wallets; Bahasa Indonesia is spoken by 270 million people. This contribution directly expanded Bitcoin self-custody to a massive underserved population.

## Contributions

- **BlueWallet Bahasa Indonesia translation** — early contributor to [BlueWallet/BlueWallet](https://github.com/BlueWallet/BlueWallet)
- Electronic engineering background relevant for Bitaxe hardware contributions

## Why This Matters

Localisation is a critically undervalued form of open-source contribution. Making Bitcoin's best mobile wallet accessible in Bahasa Indonesia reduced the barrier to self-custody for 270 million people. This is Bitcoin adoption work and open-source work simultaneously.
