## About

Specter B hosts a weekly co-working session every Monday at Bitcoin Center Seoul, records and publishes a Bitcoin podcast, runs a monthly Bitcoin self-custody workshop, and organises the largest Bitcoin conference in Korea. This sustained, multi-format community infrastructure makes Specter B one of the most active Bitcoin community builders in East Asia.

## Community Work

- **Weekly Monday co-working sessions** at Bitcoin Center Seoul
- **Monthly Bitcoin self-custody workshops** for Korean audiences
- **Korea's largest Bitcoin conference** — organiser
- **Bitcoin podcast** creator and publisher

## Why This Matters

Korea has a sophisticated developer ecosystem and 50 million people — with very low Bitcoin Core contributor representation. Specter B's infrastructure (weekly events, monthly workshops, annual conference) is a sustained funnel for converting Korean software engineers into Bitcoin contributors.
