## About

Wolf is one of Code Orange's most active community contributors in outreach and education. Having volunteered at Bitcoin House Bali and organised in-person meetups across five Indonesian cities (Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Tangerang, and Bali), Wolf has a demonstrated track record of spreading Bitcoin and Lightning Network adoption across Indonesia.

An international partnership with [Einundzwanzig Brandenburg](https://einundzwanzig.space) (Germany's Lightning community) used Blockschmiede — an LN+NFC payment application — to demonstrate real-world Lightning adoption. Wolf has completed My First Bitcoin Online three times — once as a student educator — and writes consistently about Bitcoin, circular economy, and Austrian economics.
