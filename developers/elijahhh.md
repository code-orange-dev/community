## About

Elijah co-organises [BitDevs Zambia](https://bitdevszambia.org) — a community running monthly Socratic Seminars and workshops on Bitcoin protocol development in Lusaka, inspired by the original BitDevs NYC. BitDevs Zambia officially launched in April 2025, supported by Bitcoin for Fairness and Anita Posch.

Elijah is learning Rust and building personal Lightning Network tools and applications, with a clear roadmap toward open-source contributions to Lightning infrastructure.

## Technical Roadmap

## Why This Matters

Zambia faces significant currency instability and financial exclusion. A Lightning developer in Lusaka who also runs the country's Bitcoin developer education community represents exactly the alignment of personal stake and technical contribution that produces the most committed long-term contributors. Elijah is building the infrastructure they and their community will actually use.
